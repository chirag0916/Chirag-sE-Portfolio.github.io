Message

You hired a company to install your new garage door and opener. The installation was incomplete or faulty. Naturally, you write a letter requesting that the business fulfill its obligation and complete the job as originally ordered and/or per the warranty or guarantee. Your tone is businesslike, but firm. Do not request, but do not demand, either. Give the recipient the opportunity to apologize and make amends. Using strong, emotional language and demanding tones as first contact may not work in your favour.
